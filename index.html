Directors Message

Janakpuri Super Specialty hospital Society is envisaged to be a leading super specialty health care provider. We are very new, still in our infancy, starting to take our first steps but we will soon be walking on our feet and will provide superior unparalleled quality health care services.

VISION & MISSION

Vision : Our vision is to match the existing Super Specialty Hospitals in both the public and private sectors and be a leading Super-Specialty Tertiary Care Center ourselves. We aim to incorporate state-of-the-art scientific & technological advances in health care, teaching, training and research activities which can help in the better management of patients and updating the knowledge and skills of the health care providers.

Mission : Our mission is to be a center of excellence and provide world class quality health care with dignity through a humane and tender approach as is depicted in our logo where two tender hands are holding a human being and giving care with dignity. We would like to maintain the highest possible standard for medical care.
